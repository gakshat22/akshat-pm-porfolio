## Delivery Fee Impact on Checkout Funnel  

**Situation**  
The checkout funnel had a high drop-off rate, with **Delivery Fee (DF)** suspected as a major friction point. Users displayed sensitivity to DF, but the exact pain-points and behavioral responses were not well understood.  

**Task**  
Analyze the impact of different **DF components** on the checkout funnel, identify where users were most sensitive, and propose interventions to reduce drop-offs.  

**Action**  
- Orchestrated funnel-level analysis to isolate DF as a key factor in abandonment.  
- Segmented users by their sensitivity to delivery fee components (base DF, surge charges, convenience fees).  
- Collaborated with product and operations teams to propose targeted fee adjustments and UI messaging improvements.  

**Result**  
- Reduced checkout drop-offs due to DF by **15%**, directly improving conversion rates and user satisfaction.

## B2B Cart Abandonment RCA  

**Situation**  
Cart abandonment in the B2B vertical was high, limiting revenue growth. There was limited visibility into **user cohorts** and their purchase behaviors.  

**Task**  
Conduct a **root cause analysis (RCA)** on B2B cart abandonment and propose actionable strategies to increase conversions.  

**Action**  
- Segmented B2B customers based on purchasing frequency, order value, and recency.  
- Identified high-value segments with elevated abandonment rates.  
- Worked with marketing to design precise retargeting campaigns for those cohorts.  

**Result**  
- Increased B2B cart conversion rates by **+12% within 3 months**, improving topline growth.
