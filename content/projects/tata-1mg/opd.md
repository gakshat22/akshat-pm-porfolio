## OPD Product Context (Tata 1mg)

 ### 1. Market & Need for OPD
- Corporate healthcare in India has historically centered on **IPD (In-Patient Department) hospitalization insurance**, even though **OPD (Out-Patient Department) care** — doctor consultations, diagnostics, medicines, dental, vision, mental wellness, preventive care — is far more frequently utilized.  
- Employers increasingly demand **preventive and everyday healthcare benefits** to reduce long-term medical costs and improve workforce well-being.  
- Insurers and existing providers face challenges: fragmented vendor ecosystem, heavy reliance on reimbursements, manual processes, and poor visibility/reporting.  

---

### 2. What Was Missing
- **Fragmented coverage** — most plans limit OPD to pharmacy and diagnostics.  
- **Poor user experience** — paper-based reimbursements with long settlement cycles.  
- **No real-time tracking** of entitlement or spend for corporates and employees.  
- **Weak adoption** — benefits remain underutilized despite demand.  
- **Limited innovation** — competitors lag in automation and digital-first experiences.  

---

### 3. How Tata 1mg Filled the Gap
- Built a **comprehensive OPD suite** spanning multiple services, available in both **cashless and reimbursement models**.  
- Designed a **multi-wallet entitlement system** configurable by employers across services.  
- Developed a **hybrid architecture**:  
  - **Cashless-first journeys** for pharmacy, diagnostics, and consultations.  
  - **Structured reimbursement journeys** for non-core services where vendor integration isn’t feasible.  
- Introduced **automation-first workflows** to reduce TAT, error rates, and manual dependency.  
- Rolled out after **thorough internal testing**, ensuring reliability before client launch.  
- Product is **currently functional and continuously being worked upon** to improve scalability, automation, and coverage.  

---

### 4. Product Details & Strengths
- **Coverage breadth**: Pharmacy, Diagnostics, In-clinic & Virtual Consults, Dental, Vision, Mental Wellness, Gym, Vaccinations, Ambulance.  
- **Unified wallet**: Employees access multiple services via a single entitlement wallet.  
- **Cashless integration**: Real-time wallet debits for select categories.  
- **Reimbursement innovation**: Custom-built reimbursement tool automating wallet deduction and payouts (replacing Google Sheets).  
- **Admin visibility**: Real-time dashboards for corporates.  
- **Modular architecture**: New services can be switched on without re-architecting.  
- **GenAI-powered build process**: Leveraged generative AI to:  
  - Write PRDs.  
  - Conduct market research and competitor scans.  
  - Draft wireframes and design UI components.  
  - Build simple automation workflows.  

---

- **Hybrid design** — flexible mix of cashless and reimbursement.  
- **Superior UX** — seamless digital journeys for employees.

### 7. Delivery to Clients

---

### 8. Impact (So Far)
- **Scale & Revenue**: 4 clients onboarded, covering **12,000+ employees**, with **₹32 Cr expected ARR**.  
- **Utilization**: ~30% adoption, with top usage in **pharmacy, diagnostics, and in-clinic consultations**.  
- **Ops Performance**:  
  - Average reimbursement **TAT ~4 days**.  
  - Transitioned from **manual Google Sheets → automated tool** for reimbursements.  
- **Automation Benefits**: Wallet deduction and payout flows now handled digitally, reducing manual errors.  
- **Adoption Insight**: Low reimbursement demand, indicating strong **cashless-first product-market fit**.  
- **Competitive Positioning**: No benchmarking done yet, but unique **hybrid + multi-service design** sets Tata 1mg apart.
