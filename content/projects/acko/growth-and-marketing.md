## Fixing Demand Estimation Errors  

**Situation**  
The organization’s demand numbers, a key metric for planning and forecasting, were systematically flawed due to errors in the calculation process. This created a **12% gap** between actual and reported demand, leading to misaligned decisions across teams.  

**Task**  
Identify the root cause of demand miscalculation and implement a **permanent fix** that ensures accuracy and consistency across the org.  

**Action**  
- Audited existing demand calculation pipelines across sales, product, and marketing.  
- Identified sources of error, including overlapping definitions of “active demand” and inconsistent attribution windows.  
- Proposed and initiated structural changes in how demand was computed, working with analytics and engineering stakeholders.  

**Result**  
- Eliminated **12% error in demand estimation**, creating a single source of truth.  
- Improved planning accuracy for leadership and reduced friction between teams.

## Improving Drip Marketing Returns  

**Situation**  
The drip marketing channel showed suboptimal performance, with many users ignoring or unsubscribing from messages due to poor timing and lack of personalization.  

**Task**  
Optimize **communication timing** and personalize messaging to maximize **return rates** from drip campaigns.  

**Action**  
- Conducted a time-series analysis to identify **precise communication windows** where user engagement peaked.  
- Leveraged behavioral and demographic data to personalize campaign content.  
- Partnered with CRM/marketing ops to automate segmentation and delivery.  

**Result**  
- Increased **return rate from drip marketing by +15%**, significantly boosting campaign efficiency.  This added around 3k extra customer leads per day.

## Leveraging Old Demand for New Conversions  

**Situation**  
Old demand leads were being underutilized, with limited insight into their behavioral patterns. As a result, their contribution to daily sales was lower than potential.  

**Task**  
Analyze the impact of **old demand** on sales and identify actionable ways to increase conversions.  

**Action**  
- Segmented old demand based on behavioral and demographic parameters.  
- Ran targeted experiments on re-engagement campaigns for different cohorts.  
- Provided insights to marketing teams on which segments carried the highest conversion propensity.  

**Result**  
- Improved conversion of old demand by **+5% within one month**.  
- Equipped marketing with a repeatable framework for lead reactivation.

## Automating Marketing Reporting & Dashboards  

**Situation**  
Marketing reporting was highly manual, involving fragmented reports across multiple stakeholders, leading to delays and inefficiencies.  

**Task**  
Streamline reporting processes and create a **centralized dashboard** for digital marketing channel tracking.  

**Action**  
- Automated multiple recurring reports, eliminating manual work.  
- Built a **one-stop dashboard** for comprehensive tracking of digital marketing KPIs across acquisition, engagement, and spend.  
- Trained marketing stakeholders on using the dashboard for self-serve analytics.  

**Result**  
- Reduced reporting effort by **100% (manual work eliminated)**.  
- Improved visibility and alignment across marketing, growth, and leadership teams.
